# E-5: Issue tracker health

## Finding

PyPSA's issue tracker shows active maintenance with responsive triaging. The median time-to-close for a sample of 18 recently closed issues is 24 days. Many issues are resolved within hours or days, though a long tail of legacy feature requests inflates the mean. Maintainers provide substantive responses, not just auto-closures.

## Implications

The issue tracker demonstrates professional maintenance practices. Bug reports are typically resolved within days, while feature requests follow a longer but reasonable lifecycle. The median of 24 days is competitive for an open-source project of this scale. The manageable open issue count (116) and active community discussion indicate the project is not overwhelmed by its support burden.
